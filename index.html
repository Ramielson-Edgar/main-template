How is AdroFx's client money protected?

We take responsibility for ensuring the security of clients' funds because we recognize that traders should concentrate on trading. AdroFx is extremely cautious with clients' funds during trading because it has always worked to give clients the best conditions. The security of the funds is ensured by a number of crucial elements.

  • Regulated Brokerage Company

    Financial brokerage firm AdroFx is licensed to trade in financial and commodity-based derivative instruments and other securities under the VFSC's Special Brokerage License( number 700546).

  • Negative Balance Protection

    In case the market volatility doesn't allow the client to keep a positive balance on his/her trading account, RoboForex Ltd automatically tops up the balance to 0 and the client doesn't have to pay anything for this.

  • Segregated Account

    With the funds being in a segregated account, the investor has full control of the money and can withdraw the funds at any time. Another security reason is for the ease of use between accounts

  • Security & Privacy

    We use a connection that is secured by an SSL certificate of the highest caliber for security. Additionally, we will never divulge your personal information without your consent.

  • Accounts with primary banks

    Only major international banks will be used by AdroFx. The company can offer high-quality liquidity through major banks thanks to the brand's strength and international reputation.

  • Market-leading insurance

    A civil liability insurance program with a cap of$ 500 000 USD has been put in place by AdroFx. This program offers market-leading protection against errors, negligence, fraud, omissions, and other risks that could result in client financial losses.
