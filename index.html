How clients' funds are safeguarded at RoboForex?

We understand that traders should focus on trading, therefore we take responsibility for ensuring the safety of clients' funds. RoboForex has always strived to provide clients with the best conditions, therefore, during trading, it is very careful with clients' funds. The funds' security s is guaranteed by several important factors.

  • Regulated Brokerage Company

    RoboForex Ltd is a financial brokerage company that holds a special brokerage license at FSC "Trading in financial and commodity-based derivative instruments and other securities", number 000138/437.

  • Negative Balance Protection

    In case the market volatility doesn't allow the client to keep a positive balance on his/her trading account, RoboForex Ltd automatically tops up the balance to 0 and the client doesn't have to pay anything for this.

  • Membership of the Compensation Fund of The Financial Commission

    roboForex Ltd is a member "A" of The Financial Commission. The purpose of the Commission is to allow for alternative pre-trial settlement of disputes between traders and brokerage companies.

  • Membership of The Financial Commission

    RoboForex Ltd is a member "A" of The Financial Commission. The purpose of the Commission is to allow for alternative pre-trial settlement of disputes between traders and brokerage companies.

  • Verify My Trade (VMT)

    RoboForex Ltd has received the execution quality certificate Verify My Trade (VMT), confirming its compliance with the strict requirements of the Financial Commission to its members.

  • Market-leading insurance

    RoboForex has implemented a Civil Liability insurance program for a limit of 2,500,000 EUR, which includes market-leading coverage against omissions, fraud, errors, negligence, and other risks that may lead to financial losses of clients.
